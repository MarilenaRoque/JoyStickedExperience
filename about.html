JoySticked Experience

An event specially planned for those who loves Games. The latest technologies, this year, with a special touch of nostalgia. JoySticked 2020 Edition has The Classic Games as its theme and it promises to be the best experience for the old school Games fans. It is a yearly Brazilian video game convention organized by business executive Mari Roque, that is currently held in Pinheiral and is the largest gaming convention in Latin America.
